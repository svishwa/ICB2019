ICB 2019 Venue


Conference Location

ICB 2019 Conference will be held at Aldemar Knossos Royal, Hersonissos, Crete, Greece.

Getting There

Airport

  • There are three airports in Crete: Heraklion Airport (or "Kazantzakis" airport - code: HER) Chania Airport (or "Daskalogiannis" - code: CHQ) and the small Sitia Airport (code: JSH). All of them are international airports.

  • Heraklion International Airport, “Nikos Kazantzakis” (IATA: HER, ICAO: LGIR) is the primary airport and it is approx. 28m from the Conference Venue.

Airport to Conference Venue(By Taxi)

  • Distance: 28Km

  • Time: ~30

  • Price: €30-35
